Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the Inner Strength Institute.

What is the Inner Strength Institute?

The Inner Strength Institute is an organization dedicated to reducing barriers to mental health care and combating stigma among men and boys.

Who can benefit from your services?

Our services are designed for men and boys of all ages who may be struggling with mental health challenges, such as stress, anxiety, or trauma.

Why is there a focus on men and boys?

Men often face unique challenges, including societal stigma around expressing emotions or seeking help. We aim to create a safe and supportive environment for them.

What types of services do you offer?

Our services include individual therapy, offering personalized one-on-one sessions designed to address your unique needs. Group therapy provides the opportunity to connect with a supportive community of peers navigating similar challenges. Through workshops and seminars, you can develop valuable skills such as emotional regulation, stress management, and effective communication. We also offer youth programs, providing specialized mentorship and skill-building support for boys. Additionally, our corporate wellness programs deliver focused workshops tailored for male-dominated work environments.

How do I know if therapy is right for me?

Therapy can benefit anyone facing challenges they want to understand or overcome. If you’re feeling stuck, overwhelmed, or looking for ways to improve your mental well-being, therapy can provide tools and strategies to help.

How do I schedule an appointment?

To schedule an appointment, you can call us at 833-756-8894, complete our online form, or email us at support@theinnerstrengthinstitute.com. We’ll walk you through the process and connect you with a therapist who best meets your needs.

What can I expect during my first session?

Your first session will be an opportunity to discuss your concerns and goals with your therapist. They’ll ask questions to better understand your needs and work with you to develop a plan tailored to your situation.

Do you offer virtual therapy sessions?

Yes! We provide telehealth options for those who prefer or need to meet online. Virtual sessions are confidential and conducted via a secure platform.

How much do services cost?

Costs vary depending on the type of service. We strive to keep our services affordable and may offer sliding-scale fees based on financial need. Contact us for specific pricing or to discuss payment options.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept many insurance plans. Please contact us to verify coverage or learn more about out-of-pocket costs. If you don’t have insurance, we’ll work with you to explore alternative payment options.

Can I refer someone to the Inner Strength Institute?

Absolutely! If you know someone who could benefit from our services, please encourage them to reach out. You can also contact us directly for guidance on how to make a referral.

What if I’m in crisis or need immediate help?

If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please call 911 or reach out to a crisis hotline immediately. The National Suicide Prevention Lifeline is available at 988, and you can access the Crisis Text Line by texting HOME to 741741.
